What we do.

At Artemis Endevours, we support the people stuck in abusive situations. Specifically, our speciallty is recovery of electronic devices and your online presence. Additionally we have several methods of securing your devices and accounts to protect them from further intrusion. We are here to help you get safe in relation to your digital existance.

Why we do it.

We do this because we've been there. We've had our identities stolen, our picture leaked, our social media comprimised, our selves held ransom at the mercy of our abusers. We know what it's like, and that knowing is what inspired us to create this program to begin with. To be the hand we sought in the darkness when there wasn't one there for us.
